What is VISIONSPEAK? What are we going to do in it?

Text-to-speech is an AI-based system that converts text into speech. It uses Natural Language Processing and Digital Signal Processing to achieve its goal. It is a web-based system that converts text to speech. It was developed using JavaScript and is compatible with a variety of speech synthesis libraries.

Why is it important? Why do we need it?

Text-to-speech synthesizers are indispensable for making written content accessible to diverse users. They convert text into spoken words, benefiting those with visual impairments, reading difficulties, or multitasking needs. TTS technology supports language learning, aids efficiency by enabling hands-free consumption of content, and enhances inclusivity in digital interactions. With customizable features and integration across various applications, TTS plays a pivotal role in ensuring equal access and convenience for all users.
